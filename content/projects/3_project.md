Developed an autonomous badminton training robot with multimodal perception, integrating lidar, depth cameras, and advanced planning for dynamic serving, auto-retrieval, and interactive training.

## Highlights

- Built perception system and decision-making mechanism for autonomous robot.
- Implemented Fast-LIO SLAM with mid360 lidar for mapping and localization.
- Optimized ego-planner for 2D path planning and trajectory smoothing.
- Achieved robust auto-retrieval and precise serving for closed-loop intelligent training.
