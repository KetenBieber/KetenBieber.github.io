  R2 is the shooting robot for the 2025 China National Robot Competition (Robocon), featuring high-speed omnidirectional mobility, LiDAR SLAM localization, and RGB-D camera multimodal perception. Built on a three-steering-wheel chassis with a rubber-band trigger launching mechanism, R2 executes rapid acceleration and emergency braking, over-half-court auto-aim shooting at any distance (including long-range), net interception, and full-court localization on a standard basketball field.

The R2 shooting robot for Robocon 2025, integrating EtherCAT-based lower-computer-free real-time control, three-steering-wheel chassis kinematics, LiDAR SLAM localization, and RGB-D multimodal perception. Capable of high-speed omnidirectional movement, auto-aim long-range shooting, and full-court awareness on a basketball field.
